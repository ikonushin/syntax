### Receipt Statistics
- **Count**: Total number of receipt entries created

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Why don't I see the analytics button?
**A:** The analytics button only appears after you've created at least one receipt. Create your first receipt, and the button will appear.

### Q: Why is the chart empty?
**A:** No receipts fall within the selected period. Try switching to a longer period (e.g., from "Week" to "Month" or "Year").

### Q: Can I change the date range?
**A:** Currently, only preset periods are available (Week/Month/Year). Custom date ranges will be added in a future update.

### Q: Does the data update in real-time?
**A:** The dashboard updates when you change filters. Click the "üîÑ –û–±–Ω–æ–≤–∏—Ç—å –¥–∞–Ω–Ω—ã–µ" button at the bottom to refresh manually.

### Q: How do I export this data?
**A:** Export functionality for analytics reports will be added in a future update. Currently, you can screenshot the dashboard or export individual receipts from the main screen.

### Q: What if I have no data?
**A:** Create receipts first, then return to the analytics dashboard. You need at least one receipt for the dashboard to display data.
